Description

The org.bus1.administrator is the central component of the system. It holds a direct connections to all services which are needed to keep the control of the running system. During bootup, it installs the service configurations in the org.bus1.activator(1), which is responsible to execute the services on-demand. The org.bus1.administrator activates and configures the services and maintains the overall system state according to its task.
