Description

The org.bus1.activator connects the services to the bus. It receives the configuration for the bus services from the org.bus1.administrator(1). It creates a bus peer for every configured service and listens for incoming requests on these peers. Incoming messages cause the activator to execute the service and hand over the bus connection with the queued messages.
